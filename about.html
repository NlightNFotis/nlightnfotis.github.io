A Little Bit About Me :)

Who am I?

My name is Fotis Koutoulakis. Many of you may know me as NlightNFotis or nlightnfotis. What matters about me is that I am a young (currently 21 years old) Computer Scientist from Greece.

Regarding my computer adventures, I am mostly interested in anything that has to do with computer infrastructure, such as Compilers, Operating Systems, Virtual Machines, Programming languages, and the like.

I am also a User and Developer of Free Software. I am contributing to Arch Hurd, a simple and lightweight Hurd distribution. I am also working on the Hurd and GCC (The GNU Compiler Collection).

Regarding my career plans, I am hoping to work as either a kernel hacker or a compiler engineer (or both. That sounds better :) )

Current Occupation

I am working as a Google Summer of Code 2013 intern on fully porting gccgo (the Go language frontend for gcc) and the rest of the infrastructure required to allow software written on Go to be correctly executed on the Hurd. You may find more information on my original project proposal, or under the gsoc tag in the blog posts.
